Sitting is Killing You!

Making Sitting Healthier

We built Cubii, our under-desk elliptical trainer to fight the sitting disease that is so common in today’s working lifestyle. Today, an office worker spends more than 6 hours sitting. This is known to result in obesity, diabetes, and other cardiovascular issues. Furthermore, over $1429 more is spent in healthcare cost on an obese person as compared to a person of normal weight.

Through Cubii, Fitness Cubed will significantly reduce health problems for individuals, and healthcare expenditures for employers and insurance companies.

Elliptical Redesigned

Unlike traditional standup elliptical machines, Cubii is specifically designed for use under your desk. Cubii facilitates unconscious leg movements, which provide you with basic exercise to keep blood flowing through your regular workday, without causing any distraction.

Workplace Renewed

With utility, comes design. At Fitness Cubed, we value and invest in the design and user experience of our products. We actively seek to promote this culture for our customers who use Cubii. Our sleek and contemporary design makes any workspace look one step ahead in interior aesthetics.
